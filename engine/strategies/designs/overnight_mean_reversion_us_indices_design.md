# Overnight Mean Reversion — US Indices — Design Report

## Summary

**Idea:** Overnight Mean Reversion on US100 and US500. During the Asian session the market tends to retrace strong moves from the previous US regular session (RTH). Entry at 23:00 UTC, partial exit at 07:00 UTC (London open proxy via 1.5R scale-out), final force-close at 14:25 UTC before RTH opens.

## Phase 1 — Technical Analysis

### Strategy Type
**Session-based mean reversion with time-gated intraday cycling**

Entry at 23:00 UTC. SHORT if prior RTH was bullish (>0.5% move). LONG if prior RTH was bearish (>0.5% move). ATR-based SL (2x), partial exit 50% at 07:00 UTC (European open), trailing stop after partial, final time exit at 14:25 UTC.

### Proposed Indicators

| name | type | params | purpose |
|------|------|--------|---------|
| `ema5` | `ema` | `{"period": 5}` | Short-term trend proxy (5-bar = ~5h on H1) |
| `ema20` | `ema` | `{"period": 20}` | Medium-term trend proxy (20-bar = ~20h on H1) |
| `adx14` | `adx` | `{"period": 14}` | Trend strength gate (ADX < 25 = waning trend, reversion viable) |
| `atr14` | `atr` | `{"period": 14}` | Volatility for SL/trailing sizing |
| `session_tokyo` | `session_active` | `{"session": "tokyo"}` | Asian session gate for overnight entry window |

### Entry Logic

**LONG (fade bearish momentum):**
- `session_tokyo > 0.5` (we are in Asian session window)
- `ema5 < ema20` (recent bearish momentum = fade LONG)
- `adx14 < 25` (trend is weak/waning = reversion viable)

**SHORT (fade bullish momentum):**
- `session_tokyo > 0.5` (we are in Asian session window)
- `ema5 > ema20` (recent bullish momentum = fade SHORT)
- `adx14 < 25` (trend is weak/waning = reversion viable)

### Exit Logic
- **Scale-out**: 50% at 1.5R (proxy for London open 07:00 UTC)
- **Trailing stop**: ATR(2x) on remaining 50% after scale-out
- **Time gate**: force_close at 14:25 UTC (before RTH opens)
- **Session exit**: close when session_tokyo goes inactive

### Design Concerns
1. RTH OHLC aggregation not natively supported → EMA crossover workaround
2. Partial exit at fixed UTC time requires `scale_out.trigger_time` (not yet in engine) → R-multiple proxy
3. ATR warmup 14 bars on H1 = 14 hours before first trade

## Phase 1 — Market Structure Analysis

### Regime Assessment
**Mean reversion: viable with filters**
- ADX < 25 gate required to distinguish reversion days from continuation
- Without ADX gate, strategy enters on strong trends → directional failure
- Entry at 23:00 UTC is 1-2h post-RTH; acceptable with EMA proxy for prior direction

### Instrument Suitability
**US100 (Nasdaq-100) & US500 (S&P 500):** Well-suited
- High liquidity, defined RTH session structure
- Significant Asian-session retracement patterns documented empirically
- `session_active`, `scale_out`, `trailing_sl`, `trading_hours` all engine-supported

### Filter Recommendations
1. **ADX < 25** — prevents entry on strong-trend continuation days (blocks ~30-40% of entries, raises win rate)
2. **trading_hours(23:00–14:25, force_close)** — enforces overnight window, prevents RTH bleed
3. **RTH move detection** — not natively available; EMA(5)/EMA(20) crossover used as proxy

```json
  "trailing_sl_atr_mult": 2.0,

  "time_exit_bars": 15,
  "trading_hours": { "from_time": "23:00", "to_time": "14:25", "force_close": true }
}
```

### Rationale
- **1% risk per trade**: conservative for overnight index holds (gap risk exists)
- **2x ATR SL**: adapts to overnight/early-morning volatility; prevents whipsaw exits
- **Scale-out 1.5R / 50%**: locks Asian bounce profit; remaining leg rides with trailing stop
- **Trailing 2x ATR**: post-scale, follows price to capture extended moves
- **15-bar time_exit + force_close 14:25 UTC**: dual safety net prevents RTH bleed
- **max_open_trades=1**: single session cycle, no overlap risk

### Risk Considerations
- Overnight gap risk on indices: 2x ATR SL may not contain gaps >2%
- 07:00 UTC London open slippage on partial exit
- US100/US500 correlation: both short simultaneously = correlated stop-out risk
- Estimated: win rate 52-55%, profit factor 1.3-1.5, Sharpe > 0.5 achievable

## Phase 2 — Debate

### Bull Case (Advocate)

1. **Well-defined session arbitrage window**: RTH exhaustion + overnight sentiment reset is a real market microstructure inefficiency
2. **Three-phase exit**: partial at London open (scale-out), trailing on remainder, hard close at 14:25 = asymmetric risk profile
3. **ATR-based sizing**: auto-adapts position size to volatility; bigger ATR → fewer shares, smaller ATR → more shares
4. **ADX gate**: removes ~30-40% of false entries on strong-trend continuation days
5. **Time gates**: 23:00–14:25 UTC window explicitly avoids Fed/ECB risk, weekend gaps
6. **Engine support confirmed**: ATR, ADX, session_active, scale_out, trailing_sl, trading_hours all supported

**Sharpe > 0.5 feasibility**: 52-55% win rate × 1.5R avg winner / 1.0R avg loser = profit factor ~1.3-1.5 → Sharpe > 0.5 achievable on 15-25 monthly signals

### Bear Case (Critic)

**Round 1 blocking concerns (3):**
1. RTH OHLC aggregation missing → schema violation
2. Partial exit at 07:00 UTC precision + trigger_r vs time-gate conflict
3. Multi-leg SL management beyond Phase 1 capabilities

**Round 2 final blocking (1, then resolved in composer):**
- EMA direction inversion: advocate's proposed fix had SHORT when EMA(5)<EMA(20), but correct logic is SHORT when EMA(5)>EMA(20) (fade bullish momentum). Resolved in final JSON.

**Advisory concerns (retained):**
- EMA(5) lookback = 5h; may not fully capture 8.5h RTH window; consider EMA(8)/EMA(21)
- ADX(14) may not have stabilized at 23:00 UTC entry; consider ADX(21)
- Curve-fitting risk on ADX=25 / ATR=2x thresholds; validate out-of-sample

## Engine Gaps Found During Design

- **gap**: RTH OHLC aggregation — no built-in indicator to compute prior session open-to-close return
  - **workaround**: ~~EMA(5)/EMA(20) crossover proxy~~ → **RESOLVED**: `session_return` indicator implemented and deployed. Strategy JSON updated to use `rth_return` (session_return, from_time=14:30, to_time=21:00). Gap closed 2026-03-28.
  - **priority**: ~~HIGH~~ → DONE

- **gap**: Session-time-anchored partial exit — design intent was scale-out at 07:00 UTC, not at floating 1.5R
  - **workaround**: `scale_out.trigger_r = 1.5` as profit-based proxy; may fire before or after 07:00 UTC
  - **priority**: MEDIUM
  - **suggested_fix**: Add `scale_out.trigger_time` (HH:MM UTC) as alternative/combined trigger alongside `trigger_r`

- **gap**: SL migration to breakeven after scale_out — no sequencing guarantee between scale_out and trailing_sl activation
  - **workaround**: trailing_sl always active from entry; breakeven step implicit if trailing trails from current price
  - **priority**: MEDIUM
  - **suggested_fix**: Add `breakeven_after_scale_out: true` flag; engine sets SL to entry price when scale_out triggers

- **gap**: `session_active("tokyo")` covers ~00:00-09:00 UTC; misses 23:00-00:00 UTC hour of intended window
  - **workaround**: `trading_hours.from_time = "23:00"` is the binding entry gate; session_tokyo is supplemental confirmation
  - **priority**: LOW
  - **suggested_fix**: Allow `session_active` params to accept custom `from_time`/`to_time` overrides, or add `time_window_active` indicator type
