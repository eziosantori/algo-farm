## Analysis Summary

### Technical Analysis
7-indicator breakout system with 4-layer entry confirmation:
1. Bollinger upper band breakout (volatility expansion trigger)
2. Volume spike > 20-bar average (institutional flow confirmation)
3. HTF EMA alignment (D1 for H4 variant, H4 for H1 variant)
4. ADX > 20 (momentum filter, anti-chop)

Dual exit system:
- SuperTrend flip (trend reversal detection)
- ATR trailing stop 2.0x (let winners run)

### Market Structure Analysis
**H4 IDEAL:** Fewer high-quality trades, better Sharpe potential  
**H1 MARGINAL:** Needs session filter (10:00-15:45 ET implemented)

**Asset suitability:**
- NVDA/AMD: Highly suitable (high volatility, strong trends)
- AAPL: Moderate (less volatile, may reduce trade frequency)

**CRITICAL:** Earnings protection via force-close 15:45 ET eliminates overnight gap risk

### Risk & Position Management
- **Stop:** ATR 2.0x (accommodates stock volatility)
- **Risk:** 1% per trade
- **Max trades:** 1 (prevents tech sector correlation blow-up)
- **Trading hours:** 10:00-15:45 ET (America/New_York timezone)
- **Force-close:** Enabled (eliminates earnings gap risk)

## Engine Gaps Found During Design

1. **Volume multiplier not supported**
   - **Gap:** Cannot express `volume > 1.5 * vol_sma` directly in entry rules
   - **Workaround:** Use `volume > vol_sma` (simpler threshold)
   - **Priority:** MEDIUM
   - **Suggested fix:** Add `multiplier` param to entry rule comparisons (e.g., `"compare_to": "vol_sma", "multiplier": 1.5`)

2. **Earnings calendar awareness missing**
   - **Gap:** No native earnings date detection for stock-specific position management
   - **Workaround:** Force-close all positions at 15:45 ET daily (eliminates overnight gaps)
   - **Priority:** HIGH (for stock strategies)
   - **Suggested fix:** Add optional `earnings_protection: true` flag that auto-closes 1 day before/after earnings dates

## Risk Warnings

1. **Tech sector correlation:** All 3 instruments are tech stocks. Max 1 open trade prevents simultaneous blow-up, but consider adding non-tech names (e.g., JPM, XOM) in future iterations.

2. **Earnings volatility:** Force-close at 15:45 ET eliminates overnight gap risk, but cannot prevent intraday earnings announcements (rare on these names).

3. **Volume spike interpretation:** Current implementation uses `volume > vol_sma` (fallback). If backtest shows poor selectivity, request engine support for `volume > 1.5 * vol_sma` (ENGINE_GAP #1).

4. **HTF EMA lag:** D1 EMA (H4 variant) provides strong trend confirmation but delays entries. If missing too many moves, consider switching to H1 variant or reducing HTF EMA period from 50 to 30.
