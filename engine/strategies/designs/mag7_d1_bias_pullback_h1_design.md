## Phase 1 — Technical Analysis

### Indicator Set
- Trigger TF (H1): EMA20, EMA50, RSI14, ADX14, ATR14
- D1 bias: `htf_ema` 200 (forward-filled onto H1)
- Volume confirmation: `volume_sma(1)` and `volume_sma(20)`

### Entry Logic (Long)
- D1 bias long-only: `close > d1_ema200`
- Local trend: `ema20 > ema50`
- Pullback reclaim: `close crosses_above ema20` and `close > ema50`
- Momentum and trend strength: `rsi14 > 50` and `adx14 > 20`
- Volume confirmation: `vol_sma1 > vol_sma20`

### Exit Logic (Long)
- `close crosses_below ema20` (loss of short-term trend)
- or `close crosses_below d1_ema200` (bias flip)

Short-side rules are symmetric.

### Mapping Notes
- `crosses_above` / `crosses_below` are supported by the engine.
- `htf_ema` supports `base_timeframe` for session-based assets (stocks).
